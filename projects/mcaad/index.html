MCAAD: The Word Cloud

A sculptural conversation on the American Dream

MCAAD: The Word Cloud is an interactive sculptural installation that explores the American Dream through the lens of language and community and creates a space where individual voices come together to form something greater than the sum of its parts. Located in the heart of Washington DC, this immersive experience invites visitors to contribute their own words and stories, creating a dynamic, ever-evolving conversation about what the American Dream means today.

Role: Developer (Unity, C#, Networking, Node.js, Next.js)
